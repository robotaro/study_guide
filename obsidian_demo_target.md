## Study Tips

Spaced repetition works because forgetting is not failure — it is the signal that tells your brain what to reinforce. ^spaced-repetition

Active recall beats passive re-reading every time. Close the notes, try to reproduce the idea from memory, then check.

## Common Pitfalls

### Pitfall 1 — Highlighting Instead of Thinking

Marking text with a highlighter feels productive but encodes almost nothing. Summarize in your own words instead.

### Pitfall 2 — Skipping the Basics

Advanced topics become fragile without solid foundations. If something feels shaky, go back one level.
